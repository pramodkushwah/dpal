About Us

The Ministry of Education has been working hard to develop curriculum learning standards and national assessment to emulate best global practices. The Ministry also supports the country's strategy to promote a culture of creativity and innovation through providing students with twenty-first century skills to keep pace with global developments, the promotion of education and leading it to new horizons. This platform has been developed by Ministry of Education in supporting students towards engagement in both educational and extra-curricular activities. It aims to ease the student journey into careers. It integrates the issues and solutions of all students in higher education intitutions in UAE.

Choices

With this platform, student can choose the courses and electives based on the career they want to pursue. It provides all the information about the course flexibility, exchange programs, extra-curricular clubs, internships and core competencies.

Students now should be able to begin their courses by being fully aware of what careers they can pursue, what potential alternatives they have, how they can collaborate and network with their peers in other institutions.

Progress

Student will able to log and see their progress at one place irrespective of where they have done the course with regards to their chosen careers, based on the courses they have done, electivies and exchange programs they have taken, extra-curricular activities they have been part of, and completed internships

Employment Opportunities

Student will be able to navigate easily various employment opportunities available in various sectors. They will be able to directly apply by sharing their authenticated profile and progress with the prospective recruiters.
