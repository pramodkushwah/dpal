Scholarships

Prospective students should look at list of scholarships available. Scholarships could be based on need or merit or mixed.

Some institutes offer discounts to siblings, children of staff etc.

Sponsorship from Employer, Organization and Institute is also available.

Various scholarships available with pre-requisites, eligibility, application process, selection process/criteria and other details. Please refer to the institute website for more details as some scholarships could be institute specific.
